About Us

At Foxtail we grow high quality, organic produce for our community. We are invested in the health and viability of organic farmland in our region and continue to work to maintain and improve the land we farm. We are fourth generation farmers growing on the land that has been in our family for many years. We provide perfect produce year-round, cut fresh and delivered to restaurants and homes.

Perfect Ingrients

Everything you desire

So if pesticides and fertilizers are out of the question, how to farmers ensure quality produce? Well, a little digging (pun intended) shows that it’s both an art and science. Organic farming combines natural, green products like extracts and natural substances, farmer’s intuition, and a deep understanding of methods like crop rotation and diversification. All of this is painstakingly and tirelessly taken care of so that you can grab that basket of strawberries on your way home or have that farm to table meal featuring the freshest local vegetables.
